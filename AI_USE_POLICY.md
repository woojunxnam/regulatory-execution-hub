# AI Use Policy

AI may assist source discovery, extraction candidates, comparison, drafting, coding, and test generation. It may not autonomously publish regulatory interpretation, make a final classification, declare a dossier or site ready, determine non-applicability, invent a source, or replace a human-reviewed rule.

Runtime readiness logic must be deterministic, visible, and tested. When evidence is insufficient, return an undetermined or blocked result with the missing facts rather than a confident conclusion.
