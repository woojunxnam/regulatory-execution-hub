# AGENTS.md

- Preserve the established Next.js App Router, TypeScript, and Tailwind conventions.
- Run format, lint, type-check, unit, accessibility/route, and production-build checks before a PR.
- Never invent regulatory requirements, dates, conclusions, or citations.
- Distinguish required, conditional, recommended, potentially impacted, best practice, and undetermined content.
- Keep regulatory business data and deterministic rules outside UI components.
- Every regulatory record needs official-source status, editorial-review status, and a last-verification date.
- Do not mark content human-reviewed without a review record.
- Never expose secrets or collect confidential product, patient, or proprietary dossier information.
- Treat the product as educational decision support, not a final regulatory determination or GxP system of record.
